Frequently Asked Questions


Change Teams

If you pick the wrong team when you created your account, you can change you teams. This option is under the help link in the nav bar. You are able to change team just one time


About this Site

Tool for students in BAS in Marketing & Entrepreneurship to collaborate in teams by creating posts with project ideas, modifying created posts and voting for the posts of your preference


Voting System

Vote for a post idea that you like by assigning a quantity of points. You originally have 10 points to distribute between the projects of your preference.


Editing a Post System

You can edit the current version of a post; this new edited version will be saved as the current. You can also set as current version any of the previous post versions that are listed under the post.
